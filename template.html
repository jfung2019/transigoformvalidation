Here’s what you need to know about our credit approval process
Welcome to Transigo’s B2B Pay Later Solution (and Next Steps)
Allow us to introduce ourselves. We are Transigo Inc., the credit partner of [marketplace name], and we will be providing your requested point-of-sale credit.
For the next stage of the credit approval process, we will be presenting you with a bank credentials request via Plaid. This is to allow us to make a fair assessment of your business, maximize your chances of approval, and facilitate easy repayment of any credit amount extended to you.
We understand that you may naturally have concerns about providing access to your bank credentials. We hope the following FAQ addresses your concerns.
Frequently Asked Questions About Bank Credential Authorization
1. Who is Transigo?
We are a trusted B2B marketplace partner that provides point-of-sale credit to buyers like yourself. As part of this service, we will need your permission to access your bank account information via Plaid.
2. Who is Plaid?
Plaid is a company that provides a fully secure and audited system for people to connect their bank accounts to various apps. Its services are currently being used by multi-billion-dollar companies like Venmo, Coinbase, Betterment, Acorns, Chime, and American Express.
3. Who has access to my banking credentials?
Only Plaid will maintain a record of your banking credentials. Transigo will not – and will never have – access to your banking credentials.
4. Are my banking credentials secure with Plaid?
There is a reason Plaid is used by so many major financial services companies. The company ensures your data remains safe and private with best-in-class encryption protocols, modern cloud infrastructure, 24/7 monitoring, and independent security testing. Please read more at Plaid.com/safety.
5. Why do I need to provide my banking credentials to Plaid?
In order for us to be able to extend you the requested credit amount, we will use Plaid to access your bank account for two specific purposes. The first is to view your transaction history to properly assess your credit request. The second is to initiate an automatic ACH transfer for repayment of the extended credit amount at the end of the pre-agreed term.
6. Can Plaid or Transigo withdraw funds from my bank account without my knowledge?
Absolutely not. You will know the exact amount that needs to be repaid at the end of the credit term upon approval of the credit amount. We will also notify you X days in advance of the upcoming ACH transfer. No other funds will be transferred, and the authorization of your banking credentials will automatically be removed after the repayment is complete.
7. Will my credit information and transaction history be shared with any third parties?
The only third parties that will have access to your banking information are those working together with Transigo to provide point-of-sale credit to buyers like yourself. These include credit insurance providers like Euler Hermes and payment API providers like Dwolla – all established companies with strong reputations in the industry.
